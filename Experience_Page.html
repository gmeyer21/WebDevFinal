IT Security Intern,

Belle Tire - Allen Park, MI

My first Summer internship was at the corporate headquarters of Belle Tire near Detroit, Michigan. I remember the interview was intense, as they had brought the full IT team on for my interview, (bandwidth was definitely an issue here). I was able to make amazing connections and friendships that continued outside of work as well. This was also my first look at a hybrid schedule. In this hybrid schedule I could stay home 2-3 days a week and go to the office the other days. I had two main mentors during this time. Both of whom would help me with my final project for the team. One was a network engineer, and the other, the IT director. They helped me outline security between 3rd party applications and partners that the company uses. I also had the opportunity to meet the other interns, go on trips to stores across Michigan, a Kalamazoo trip, and a Tigers game! They also sponsored my trip to Grrcon (a hacking convention) 2 months after I had left! In the end, I presented the final project to the CEO and board of directors. The goodbyes were tough, as it had been one of the best Summers I could have asked for.
